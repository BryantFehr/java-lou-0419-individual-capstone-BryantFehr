The Superest Sightings Website On the Interwebs

Welcome to “The Superest Sightings Website On the Interwebs!” Here at TSSWOTI we don’t concern ourselves with fancy layouts or dynamic backgrounds *cough*Brandon*cough*. We’re here to do one thing. Store data on your favorite super heroes and super villains! You know of a super organization that isn’t in the database? Add it in! A new hero arrives and isn’t in the database? Put em in! A super villain successfully does an experiment to give himself a new power? Edit that villain and give him the new power! The possibilities are endless!

In addition to storing information on your favorite heroes and villains, we also keep in our database a list of sightings! If you see a super hero and super villain duking it out be sure to add it into the database!

A Few notes on sightings:

A sighting must have a location. If you do not see a location for the sighting you may need to add it into the database first!

Also, since sightings must have a location, if you delete a location it will delete the sightings associated with it. So if a super battle destroys Central Park don’t delete Central Park, just edit it and put in the description that it was destroyed!
